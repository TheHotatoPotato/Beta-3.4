Dell Laptop That Can Do Everything

CPU: INTEL CORE i5-8305G GRAPHICS: RADEON RX VEGA M GL RAM: 8GB DDR4
STORAGE: 256GB PCIE SSD
PRICE: $1500
In this video, Linus talks about this laptop which can "do everything". He explains that it's not a gaming laptop so curb expectations in this regard but the gaming on this laptop is like night and day difference compared to Intel's own integrated graphics. Esports can break 60 fps but there may be heat issues when the high-performance load is placed on it and clock speed can be reduced.
